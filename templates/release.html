How and When Reports Are Released

The truth matters — but timing can be everything.

Posterity Log is designed for a future reckoning. We do not release reports while authoritarian threats remain. All submissions are sealed until it is safe — or until 4 July 2100.

Your Control

When you submit a report, we generate multiple versions — from heavily sanitized to near-complete — and propose a release schedule for each. You’ll always see these versions first, and can approve or reject each stage.

  • 🔒 Fully Redacted: No identifying details — for early public release and analysis.
  • 🕵️‍♂️ Lightly Redacted: Some context preserved — for release once threats diminish.
  • 📜 Full Disclosure: Original report — only released once it's safe or at a future date you choose (e.g. 4 July 2100).

Why the Delay?

Releasing too early risks retaliation. Many reports concern people still in power — or systems still weaponized against dissent. We wait until exposure can help, not harm.

Trigger Conditions for Release

Some versions may be scheduled for automatic release if certain conditions are met:

  • ✅ A new administration takes office
  • ✅ Congressional inquiries begin
  • ✅ Public safety outweighs secrecy
  • ✅ You consent to earlier release

We safeguard the record until the world is ready for it.
And when that day comes — the truth will speak for itself.
